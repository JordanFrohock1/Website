NBA Win Prediction Model (Python)

Description:

This is my Data Science undergraduate capstone project where I created a Stacked Ensemble Model (a model built off advanced models) to predict the winner of an NBA game. I used a dataset of 20 NBA regular season game by game data and 20+ features to train the model. Most of the highly predictive features I created myself.I also tested the model against the models made by my collleagues at ESPN and it acheived an acuracy score nearly the same and better than other competitors. than all of them! I am super proud of this project and it is one of my favorite projects that I have worked on.
